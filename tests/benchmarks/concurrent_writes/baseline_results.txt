=== KEY OBSERVATIONS ===

Peak throughput: 59,106 inserts/s at 32 threads (591 txns/s)
Throughput DROPS at 64T (43,836) and 128T (36,152) — negative scaling due to lock contention

WAL lock contention dominates at high thread counts:
  At 1T:   seq lock   103us, compress 26us, io 18us (lock is 70% of WAL write time)
  At 64T:  seq lock  3808us, compress 33us, io 14us (lock is 99% of WAL write time)  [~14000 writes]
  At 128T: seq lock  6319us, compress 41us, io 16us (lock is 99% of WAL write time)  [~15000 writes]

Commit path bottleneck at 7000 commits (64T+128T runs):
  wal_write:     8,525us (37% of commit time)
  status_write:  3,564us (15% of commit time)
  sync_wait:     6,133us (27% of commit time)
  → WAL lock contention (wal_write + status_write) = 52% of commit time

Isolation validation also grows but is secondary:
  At 1T:   validate    1us
  At 64T+: validate 1869us

At 128T:
  commit: 214,486us avg (64.3% of total tx time)
  Performance degrades to worse than 16T throughput
